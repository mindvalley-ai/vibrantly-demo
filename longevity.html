The Energy Molecule

NAD+ Optimization

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is the master molecule of cellular energy. It declines ~50% by age 50 and is linked to virtually every hallmark of aging.

Your Current NAD+ Support üîã

You have foundational NAD+ support through your current stack:

Direct supporters: B vitamins (precursors), NAC (preserves NAD+), Berberine (activates sirtuins that use NAD+)

Consider adding: NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) 500-1000mg or NR (Nicotinamide Riboside) 300-500mg for direct NAD+ precursor support. These are the most efficient ways to boost cellular NAD+ levels.

Ages 20-30

Peak NAD+ levels. Cells have abundant energy for repair and regeneration.

Ages 40-50

NAD+ declines ~50%. Energy, recovery, and cellular repair begin to slow.

Your Position (49)

Critical window for intervention. Supporting NAD+ now can significantly impact healthspan trajectory.

Ages 60+

Without intervention, NAD+ drops to 10-25% of youthful levels. Supplementation becomes even more important.
